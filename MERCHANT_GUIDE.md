# DukanDost Pro — Merchant Onboarding Guide

Welcome to the future of business management! DukanDost Pro is designed to replace your old registers and multiple apps with a single, intelligent cockpit.

## 🚀 Getting Started (Quick Start)

### 1. Setup Your Shops
Go to **Settings > Shops** and add your main branch and any additional locations. DukanDost Pro is built for multi-branch scaling.

### 2. Import/Add Customers
Go to **Digital Khata** to add your first set of customers. 
- Enter their name and phone number.
- Setup an initial balance if they have existing udhaar.

### 3. Record Your First Sale
Go to **GST Billing > New Bill**.
- Select the customer.
- Add products (you can add new products on the fly).
- Click **Create Invoice** — a professional PDF is generated instantly!

### 4. Automated Reminders
Switch to the **Business Plan** to enable automated WhatsApp reminders. 
- Our AI will automatically identify overdue payments.
- It will send professional reminders at 11:00 AM every day.

## 📊 Understanding Your AI Dashboard

- **Business Health Score**: A score out of 100 representing your business's overall vitality. Aim for >80.
- **Credit Grade**: Your eligibility for future working capital financing.
- **Smart Tips**: Check the AI Intelligence page for personalized advice on how to increase sales or reduce risk.

## 📱 Mobile Sharing
For every invoice, you'll see a **WhatsApp Share** button. Use this to send digital bills to your customers instantly.
